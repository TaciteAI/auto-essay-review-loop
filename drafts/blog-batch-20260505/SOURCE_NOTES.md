## From Citrini "The 2028 Global Intelligence Crisis"

### Core mechanism (the displacement spiral)
- "AI capabilities improved → companies needed fewer workers → white collar layoffs increased → displaced workers spent less → margin pressure pushed firms to invest more in AI" — self-reinforcing, no natural brake
- "Ghost GDP": output in national accounts that never circulates because machines don't consume; velocity of money flatlines

### Friction elimination
- Real estate commissions: 5–6% → under 1% as agents disintermediate
- DoorDash/Uber Eats margin collapse from agent-optimized delivery
- Travel booking, insurance renewals: intermediation layers compress

### Income concentration / spending
- Top 10% earn over 50% of consumer spending
- 2% employment loss at top yields 3–4% discretionary spending decline
- Labor share of GDP: 64% (1974) → 56% (2024) → 46% (2028 projection)

### Predicted timeline
- Late 2025: agentic coding tools step-function
- 2026 Q3: ServiceNow announces 15% headcount cut
- Early 2027: consumer agents control purchasing; card interchange under pressure
- Q1 2027: Mastercard rev growth slows to 3.4%
- Q3 2027: jobless claims surge to 487K (highest since Apr 2020)
- Sept 2027: Zendesk defaults on $5B direct lending facility
- Nov 2027: market crash; perception flips from cyclical to systemic
- June 2028: unemployment 10.2%; S&P down 38% from Oct 2026 peak

### Daisy chain

### Key quotes

## Style rules for all 40 drafts

- 500–800 words. Hard floor 500 (verification check enforces).
- Blog markdown structure: 1 H1 (`#`) + 2–4 H2s (`##`). No skipping levels.
- At least 2 inline links to credible sources. Use real URLs from cyberpunk essay (Fortune, CNBC, Epoch AI, Yale, MIT, FTC, Sage, etc.).
- Plain markdown only. No emojis. No hashtags. No @-mentions.
- Voice: dispassionate, data-led, slightly polemical. Match cyberpunk-ai-essay.md tone.
- Open with a punchy single-sentence stat or claim. Don't bury the lede.
- Close with a forward-looking sentence or pointed question — never a generic CTA.
- For Chinese versions: native phrasing, NOT direct translation. Use 中文行文逻辑 — shorter sentences, fewer subordinate clauses, more concrete examples. Replace English-specific cultural references with comparable Chinese context where natural (e.g., 新一线城市 instead of "Sun Belt").
- Each draft must be self-contained. A reader who hasn't read the source essays should still get the full argument.
