## Cyberpunk-side (1–10)

1. **compute-moat** — Why frontier AI is a $660B/year hyperscaler-only club, and what "Barriers to entry" actually means at sovereign scale.
2. **on-ramp-collapse** — AI doesn't kill your job; it kills the path to your *first* one. CS grads at 7.0% unemployment.
3. **so-so-automation** — Acemoglu's textbook case: AI replaces the junior, doesn't 10x the senior, savings flow to capital.
4. **algorithmic-management** — From Amazon's Manesar warehouse to your Slack: the scanner is being ported to the keyboard.
5. **surveillance-pricing** — "Perfect price discrimination" was a theory limit. AI made it a checkout flow.
6. **antitrust-lag** — Why the DOJ takes 5 years and the next monopoly is being capitalized today.
7. **personal-ai-exit-ramp** — Own the weights or pay rent forever. The architectural choice of the decade.
8. **deepseek-trajectory** — $5.6M frontier-tier proves the moat leaks; what that buys you and what it doesn't.
9. **context-ownership** — The real moat isn't the model — it's the chat history they won't let you export.
10. **composition-over-subscription** — A constellation of small specialized models beats one $200/month oracle.

## 2028-GIC-side (11–20)

11. **displacement-spiral** — A feedback loop with no natural brake: layoffs → less demand → more AI investment → more layoffs.
12. **ghost-gdp** — Output that shows up in GDP but never circulates. Velocity of money flatlines while nominal growth looks fine.
13. **friction-elimination** — When agents do the booking, real-estate commissions go from 5–6% to under 1%. DoorDash margin collapse.
14. **top10-spending-collapse** — The top 10% earn 50%+ of consumer spending. A 2% job loss there = 3–4% spending decline.
15. **daisy-chain** — Software LBOs, life insurer annuities, and mortgage books all bet the same way on white-collar productivity.
16. **2028-mortgage-crisis** — $13T residential mortgages structurally impaired. Loans good on day one — the world changed after.
17. **interchange-pressure** — Mastercard at 3.4% growth, Visa under siege as agents route around card rails.
18. **zendesk-template** — Moat collapse signature: ARR slows, AI customer service eats the SaaS, PE owners take the haircut.
19. **revenue-paradox** — Government must transfer more *while* collecting less. Politics breaks before the math does.
20. **labor-share-46** — From 64% of GDP in 1974 to 46% by 2028. The compute owners' share is where it goes.
